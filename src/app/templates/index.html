Setup

The client

It's recommended to download and launch the client in an empty directory since it will create other files and folders to function.
The client has been tested on Windows (Windows 10/11) and Linux (MX Linux 21, Debian) with Amazon Corretto 11.
The client doesn't work on ARM processors. On Windows, if you get the error 'Can't find dependent libraries' you need msvcr100.dll.

Client login

To login into the client, you first need to register to the website.
Once registered, login and go to your profile. There you will find your GUID to login into the client.
Launch the client and click on the top left corner where you see the word 'Guest'. On the menu, click on 'Add User' and paste your GUID in 'Type your UID' and press enter on your keyboard.
You are now logged into the client and can play the challenge maps by clicking on the big blue star and then on 'play'.
Launching the game for the first time will create multiple files and folders in the client's path. Don't delete them since the client needs them to run properly.

Add a map

To add a map, look for the folder 'Songs' inside the client's path (if you don't see the folder, launch the game and it should be created. If it's still not showing, create it yourself).
Download the zip file of the map from the table above and extract its content inside the "Songs" folder you located previously.
The next time you launch the client the map will be loaded and you'll be ready to play it.
If you have too many maps in your play window, just start typing the name of the map you need and the client will filter it for you.
